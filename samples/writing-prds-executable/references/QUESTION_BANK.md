# PRD question bank (ask up to 5 at a time)

Use these when critical info is missing. Pick the smallest set that unblocks progress.

Direction
1) Who is the primary user and what job are they trying to get done?
2) What is the problem in one sentence? What happens today?
3) What does success look like (metric or observable outcome)?
4) What is the deadline / milestone that matters?
5) What constraints exist (platform, policy, privacy, budget, headcount)?

Scope and tradeoffs
6) What is explicitly out of scope for v1?
7) What is the smallest lovable version?
8) What are the key risks (technical, adoption, legal)?
9) What dependencies or teams must be involved?

AI/LLM-specific
10) What outputs are acceptable vs unacceptable? Give 3 examples each.
11) What failure is most harmful (hallucination, toxicity, privacy leak, bias, etc.)?
12) What are 5 test cases we can add immediately (happy + edge + adversarial)?
13) What are required guardrails (refusal, citations, uncertainty)?

Shipping
14) How will we roll out (beta, experiment, staged)? What is rollback?
15) What do support and marketing need to know?
