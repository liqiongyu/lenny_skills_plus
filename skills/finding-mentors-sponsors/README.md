# Finding Mentors & Sponsors (Skill Pack)

Build a Mentor & Sponsor Plan Pack (mentor portfolio, sponsor strategy, target list, outreach scripts, meeting agenda, tracking + cadence).

## What you get
- A concrete workflow to turn “I need a mentor/sponsor” into **copy/paste-ready artifacts**
- Templates for targeting, warm intros, outreach, first meetings, follow-ups, and relationship tracking
- Checklists + rubric to keep outputs **specific, respectful, and action-oriented**

## Install

This repo’s canonical skills live in `skills/`. For tool auto-discovery, this pack is mirrored to:
- `.codex/skills/finding-mentors-sponsors`
- `.claude/skills/finding-mentors-sponsors`

## How to use

Example prompts:
1) “Build me a mentor + sponsor plan. Ask me 5 questions first, then produce the full pack.”
2) “Write 3 outreach messages to approach potential mentors with humility and specificity. Include follow-ups.”
3) “Help me identify sponsors at my company and draft concrete sponsor asks tied to my current projects.”
